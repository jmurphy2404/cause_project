Support Us

A little donation goes a long way. Read below to find out exactly what our donations are used for.

With your help...

We can make a difference! Our organization forwards all donations directly to those on the ground working to protect African elephants. Whether it's supplying the park rangers with supplies and equipment, providing veterinarian care to sick or injured elephants, or building fences around a preserve, 100% of every donation goes towards supporting the cause. As shown below, donations of any amount help further our cause, and you can rest easy knowing your generosity is not being diverted.
